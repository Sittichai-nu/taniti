Getting Here & Around
Arriving on Taniti

✈ By Air

Taniti is served by a small airport accommodating small jets and propeller planes. Almost all visitors arrive by air. The airport is being expanded to accommodate larger jets within the next few years. A rental car agency is located near the airport.

⚓ By Cruise Ship

A small cruise ship docks in Yellow Leaf Bay once per week for one night. Taniti City and Merriton Landing are both walkable from the dock area. Cruise passengers have access to all island attractions during their stay.
